About BAH Travel

Dedicated to making travel seamless — from visa processing and international flights to meaningful pilgrimage experiences.

Our Journey

Founded with deep roots in pilgrimage travel, BAH Travel has grown into a full-service travel agency. Today we serve thousands of clients across the UK and Gambia with expert visa processing, competitive flight bookings, and our renowned Hajj & Umrah packages.

Our partnerships span airlines, hotels, and consulates worldwide. Whether you need a Schengen visa, a last-minute flight to London, or a five-star Hajj experience, every detail is handled by our experienced team.
